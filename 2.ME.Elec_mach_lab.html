SYLLABUS

        At least 6 (six) of the following experiments are to be conducted.
1. Study of the characteristics of a separately excited DC generator.
2. Study of the characteristics of a DC motor

5. Speed control of 3 phase Induction motor by different methods & their comparison.
6. Determination of regulation of Alternator by Synchronous Impedance method.
7. Determination of equivalent circuit parameters of a single phase motor.
8. Load test of single phase Induction motor to obtain the performance characteristics.
